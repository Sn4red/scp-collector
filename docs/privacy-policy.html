SCP Collector

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SCP Collector gathers, uses, and stores data when users interact with it.

Information Collected and Purpose

SCP Collector obtains and processes the following information from Discord users:

  • User ID: the unique identifier assigned to a user by Discord. It is used to manage gameplay features such as collecting cards, trading cards, tracking levels, experience points, and other game-related data.
  • Username: another unique identifier assigned by the user. It is used only for display purposes within the bot, such as showing usernames during trades or user card displays.
  • Command usage: only for commands that have an inactivity timeout or when they trigger an error, also collecting the user ID and username in order to keep a record of these events and understand bot usage for continuous improvement.

No other personal information is collected and SCP Collector does not use collected data for advertising, profiling, or tracking outside of gameplay functionality.

Data Storage

SCP Collector stores gameplay-related data, including the information listed above, in Google Firestore, a database service provided by Google. However, information related to command usage is not stored in Firestore. Command usage data is stored locally on the host and is retained only for a short period of time before being deleted.

Data Sharing

SCP Collector does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

User data may be processed through services required to operate the bot, such as Google Firestore, which is solely as a database hosting service. These services process data only as necessary to provide the functionality of SCP Collector and do not receive the data for independent use.

Some operational logs (such as command usage or error logs) may be stored temporarily on the host system for debugging or maintenance purposes and are automatically deleted after a short period of time.

Data Retention

User data may be retained as long as it is necessary for SCP Collector to provide its functionality. If a user stops interacting with the bot, their stored game data may remain in the database unless manually removed by the developer.

User Rights

Users may request the deletion of their stored data by contacting the developer of the bot. Upon request, reasonable efforts will be made to remove the user's stored information from the database.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, you may contact the developer through Discord or through this Google Form .
